Welcome to Blankit

Real-time PII redaction for your AI conversations

⚙️

How It Works

Blankit sits between you and your AI assistant. It automatically detects and redacts sensitive information before it reaches the cloud, and restores it in responses so your workflow stays seamless. Click on the icon on the bottom-right to toggle it on and off (or do so from the toolbar).

🔒

100% Local Processing

All redaction happens entirely in your browser. No data is sent to external servers. No analytics. No tracking. Your information never leaves your device.
